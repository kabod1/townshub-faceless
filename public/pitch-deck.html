The Problem We Solve

Voiceover costs eat into margins for solo operators who cannot record themselves.

No unified workspace exists that handles the full production pipeline for AI-assisted faceless content.

Our solution: One platform, every tool, built specifically for faceless YouTube operations.

Why ElevenLabs — Intended Integration

Townshub Faceless Video Studio integrates ElevenLabs AI voiceover directly into the content production pipeline. After a script is generated, creators can instantly synthesise a studio-quality voiceover using their chosen ElevenLabs voice — removing the final barrier to fully automated faceless video production. This closes the loop: idea → script → voiceover → thumbnail → upload, all within one platform. The ElevenLabs grant would allow us to offer premium voice generation to our users at scale and deeply embed voice AI as a core feature.
